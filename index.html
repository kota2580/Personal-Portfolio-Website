Modularized MLP-Mixer for Cancer Image Classification

PyTorch · Deep Learning · Image Augmentation

Worked on the design and evaluation of a Modularized MLP-Mixer architecture to classify histopathological images of lung and colon cancer into five categories.

  • Contributed to dataset preprocessing, image augmentation, and model performance evaluation.
  • Implemented techniques like Cosine Annealing LR, mixed-precision training etc to enhance classification accuracy.
  • Final model achieved 98.76% accuracy, outperforming ResNet-50, ViT-B/16 in both precision and efficiency.
  • Co-authored and presented the paper at ICETCI 2025 (IEEE Conference) held at Mahindra University.
GitHub
